Saudi Arabia's Role in Defense

Exploring Saudi Arabia's leadership and contributions to global defense and innovation.

Driving Innovation

Saudi Arabia is at the forefront of defense innovation, with a vision to create a self-reliant and advanced defense industry. Through initiatives like SAMI (Saudi Arabian Military Industries), the nation has made significant strides in enhancing local production and fostering international collaborations.

World Defense Show

The World Defense Show, hosted in Saudi Arabia, is a testament to the nation's commitment to bringing together the global defense community. This event highlights cutting-edge technologies, strategic partnerships, and the latest advancements in defense capabilities.

A Vision for the Future

Aligned with Vision 2030, Saudi Arabia is shaping its defense industry to become one of the top global players, focusing on sustainability, innovation, and empowerment of the next generation of leaders.
