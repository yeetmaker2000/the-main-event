Saudi Arabia's Role in Defense

Exploring leadership and innovation in shaping the future of global defense.

Driving Innovation

Saudi Arabia is at the forefront of defense innovation, with initiatives such as SAMI (Saudi Arabian Military Industries) paving the way for advanced technologies and local production capabilities. This effort strengthens both the nation’s self-reliance and its contribution to global defense solutions.

World Defense Show

A cornerstone of Saudi Arabia's vision for global collaboration, the World Defense Show is a platform that unites industry leaders, showcases cutting-edge advancements, and highlights the nation’s commitment to being a leader in defense technology and strategy.

A Vision for the Future

Aligned with Vision 2030, Saudi Arabia is shaping its defense sector to focus on innovation, sustainability, and empowerment. By investing in education, partnerships, and groundbreaking initiatives, the nation is creating a path toward a secure and progressive future for the world.
